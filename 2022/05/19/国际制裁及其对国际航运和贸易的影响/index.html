国际制裁及其对国际航运和贸易的影响

1.Sanction制裁

主要是为了应对以下事项：

  • 恐怖主义terrorism

  • 有组织的犯罪organized crime

  • 违反人权和压迫性政治制度oppressive political regimes and human rights violations

  • 侵吞其他国家领土annexation of a foreign territory

  • 大规模杀伤性武器proliferation of weapons of mass destruction (WMD)

有以下措施：

  • 武器禁运
  • 金融制裁
  • 贸易和经济制裁
  • 旅行禁令

2.USA Sanctions

2.1分类

第一种：Primary Sanctions主要制裁

针对的是US Persons(Prohibit US persons from engaging in certain activities with their counterparts in sanctioned country)，US Persons具体涉及的范围：

i.Any US citizens;
ii.Permanent resident alien;
iii.Entity organized under the laws of US or any jurisdiction within US (including foreign branches); or
iv.Any person (an individual or entity) in US

第二种：Secondary Sanctions次级制裁

比如针对的是伊朗、朝鲜、俄罗斯、叙利亚、委内瑞拉等国家（Against Iran, North Korea, Russia, Syria,Venezuela…）

  • Prohibit non-US persons from participating in or facilitating significant transactions outside US jurisdictionwith certain named individuals and organizations in sanctioned countries
  • Also apply to entities directly or indirectly owned 50% or more in the aggregate by one or more blocked persons

对于第二点，50%的规则需要注意的是：

如果一家公司/实体主要股东/个人持股超过50%，而这主要股东/个人是被制裁的，这家公司/实体也同样视为被制裁的对象

举例子-三种情况

第三种情况是：66% x 49.9% + 35%=54.76%

5.航运实务

OFAC网站上对于中远旗下的船员和管理公司的制裁，是否会影响整个集团的回答

804.Do sanctions on COSCO Shipping Tanker (Dalian) Seaman & Ship Management Co. apply to its corporate parent and affiliates?

COSCO Shipping Tanker (Dalian) Seaman & Ship Management Co. was determined by the Secretary of State on September 25, 2019, to meet the criteria for the imposition of sanctions under Executive Order (E.O.) 13846, and the Secretary of State imposed certain sanctions, including blocking, on this entity. The blocking sanctions apply only to this listed entity and any entities in which it owns, directly or indirectly, a 50 percent or greater interest. Sanctions do not apply to this entity’s ultimate parent, COSCO Shipping Corporation Ltd. (COSCO). Similarly, sanctions do not apply to COSCO’s other subsidiaries or affiliates (e.g., COSCO Shipping Holdings), provided that such entities are not owned 50 percent or more in the aggregate by one or more blocked persons. U.S. persons, therefore, are not prohibited from dealing with COSCO, its non-blocked subsidiaries, or non-blocked affiliates to the extent the proposed dealings do not involve any blocked person, or any other activities prohibited pursuant to any OFAC sanctions authorities.[January 31, 2020]


保险中的标准制裁条款：

“No insurer shall be deemed to provide cover and no insurer shall be liable to pay any claim or provide any benefit hereunder to the extent that the provision of such cover, payment of such claim or provision of such benefit would expose that insurer to any sanctions, prohibition or restriction under United Nations resolutions or the trade or economic sanctions, laws or regulations of the European Union, Japan, United Kingdom or United States of America.”

3.航运最近涉及制裁最具有参考性的case:MUR Shipping BV v RTI Ltd [2022] EWHC 467 (Comm),这里详细介绍下：

船东注册在荷兰，租家注册在美国泽西，于2016年签订包租合同COA，合同规定美元支付，且有一个不可抗力条款，最后一条规定触发不可抗力的条件：It cannot be overcome by reasonable endeavors from the Party affected.

之后租家的母公司被OFAC制裁，船东援引不可抗力，解除COA合同。租家辩称，美元支付不了，可以支付欧元-此举是reasonable endeavors，且制裁并不影响货物的装运。

法官反驳了这一点：租家支付欧元是reasonable endeavors,但并不一定就让船东接受合同未规定的欧元付款。当然这里法院也区分了合约受阻于不可抗力的不同。因此判决的理由如下（可参考下，训练逻辑用）：

  • Charterers were required to pay in US dollars under the contract and owners were entitled to require performance of the contract in accordance with its terms.

  • Reasonable endeavours did not mean endeavours towards a performance aimed at achieving a result which was not part of the parties’ agreement.

  • Reasonable endeavours did not extend to requiring owners to accept payment in a non-contractual currency,ieEUR(€), instead of US$.

  • Charterers’ alternative argument on causation: A claim for force majeure was not possible if it involved, as one aspect of causation, a decision by the party relying upon the clause

  • The court said a reasonable decision in response to a force majeure event did not disentitle a party from relying upon a force majeure clause

  • The relevant question is “whether the decision is such as to break the chain of causation from the original force majeure event”, which will usually require unreasonable conduct

  • Charterers’ attempt to rely on Owners’ refusal to accept payment in Euros did not assist because of the finding that exercise of reasonable endeavours did not require Owners to accept a non-contractual payment


航运里面经常用到的逃避制裁的方法：
  • 有关船舶和货物文件的造假
  • ship to ship transfer在海上船舶之间转移货物
  • 关掉AIS
  • 船舶进行更改船名

可以做的风控措施
  • 保险；
  • 确定货物原产地；
  • 加强反洗钱合规；
  • 监督船舶AIS状况；
  • 船舶文件的合规；
  • KYC操作；
  • 条款的拟定以及国际合作方有关制裁方案；
